# HR Policy Document: Performance Management Policy
## Document ID: HRP-004 | Version: 2.0 | Effective: April 1, 2024

---

## 1. OVERVIEW

TechCorp's Performance Management System (PMS) is a continuous process designed to align individual goals with organizational objectives, develop employee capabilities, and reward high performance. This policy covers all confirmed employees (not applicable during probation, which has a separate review process).

---

## 2. PERFORMANCE CYCLE

| Phase | Timeline | Activity |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Goal Setting | April 1 - April 30 | Set OKRs/KPIs for the year |
| Mid-Year Review | October 1 - October 31 | Progress check; goals can be modified |
| Year-End Review | January 15 - February 28 | Self-assessment + Manager rating |
| Calibration | March 1 - March 20 | HR calibration across teams |
| Rating Communication | March 25 - March 31 | Final ratings shared with employees |
| Increment & Bonus | April 1 | Increments effective; bonuses paid |

---

## 3. GOAL SETTING (OKRs)

### 3.1 OKR Framework
- **O = Objectives:** Qualitative, aspirational goals (2-5 per year)
- **KR = Key Results:** Measurable outcomes linked to each objective (3-5 per objective)
- Goals must be: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound (SMART)

### 3.2 Goal Categories
- **Business Goals (60% weightage):** Revenue targets, project delivery, customer satisfaction
- **People Goals (20% weightage):** Team development, collaboration, mentoring
- **Personal Development Goals (20% weightage):** Skill building, certifications, innovation

### 3.3 Goal Approval
- Employee drafts goals on HRMS → Manager reviews and approves → HR validates alignment
- Deadline: April 30th (Late submission = automatic "Needs Improvement" flag on goal-setting)

## 4. PERFORMANCE RATINGS

### 4.1 Rating Scale

| Rating | Score | Description | Expected Distribution |
|--------|-------|-------------|----------------------|
| Exceptional | 5 | Consistently exceeds all expectations; top 5% performer | 5% of team |
| Exceeds Expectations | 4 | Regularly exceeds most expectations | 20% of team |
| Meets Expectations | 3 | Delivers on all commitments; solid contributor | 60% of team |
| Partially Meets | 2 | Falls short in key areas; improvement needed | 10% of team |
| Does Not Meet | 1 | Significant gaps; PIP required | 5% of team |

**Bell Curve Note:** Calibration follows approximate distribution. Managers cannot rate more than 25% of team at 4 or 5 combined without HR justification.

### 4.2 Rating Process
1. **Self-Assessment:** Employee rates own performance with evidence (15 Jan - 31 Jan)
2. **Manager Assessment:** Manager rates independently, then reviews self-assessment (1 Feb - 15 Feb)
3. **Skip-Level Review:** Senior manager reviews and provides input (15 Feb - 25 Feb)
4. **Calibration Session:** HR facilitates cross-team calibration (1 Mar - 20 Mar)
5. **Final Rating:** Approved by HR Director; communicated to employee

### 4.3 What Managers Must Assess
- **Output:** Quality and quantity of work delivered
- **Behavior:** How the work was done (values alignment, collaboration)
- **Impact:** Business outcomes achieved
- **Growth:** Learning and development over the year

## 5. PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT PLAN (PIP)

### 5.1 PIP Triggers
- Rating of 1 (Does Not Meet Expectations)
- Two consecutive ratings of 2 (Partially Meets Expectations)
- Disciplinary action requiring behavior improvement

### 5.2 PIP Structure
- **Duration:** 30 days (critical cases) to 90 days (standard)
- **Content:** Specific, measurable targets with clear timelines
- **Support:** Manager meets weekly; optional HR presence
- **Monitoring:** Written progress updates bi-weekly

### 5.3 PIP Outcomes
- **Successful completion:** Returns to normal performance track; rating adjustment possible
- **Unsuccessful completion:** Termination of employment with notice pay
- **Employee rights during PIP:** Cannot be transferred, promoted, or given new projects during PIP

---

## 6. PROMOTION POLICY

### 6.1 Promotion Eligibility
- Minimum 18 months in current grade
- Minimum 2 consecutive years of rating 3 or above (OR 1 year with rating 4/5)
- No active PIP or disciplinary action in last 24 months
- Business case from manager (role availability and business need)

### 6.2 Promotion Cycle
- **Regular promotions:** April 1st (aligned with increment cycle)
- **Out-of-cycle promotions:** Possible for exceptional cases; requires CHRO approval
- **Fast-track promotion:** Possible after 1 year for exceptional (rating 5) performers

### 6.3 Promotion Increment
- **Standard promotion:** 15-20% salary increase
- **Cross-band promotion (e.g., L2 to L3):** Minimum 20% increase
- **Promotion to management (IC to people manager):** Minimum 20-25% increase

## 7. RECOGNITION PROGRAMS

### 7.1 Spot Awards
- **Frequency:** Anytime, throughout the year
- **Givers:** Any manager (L3+)
- **Award Types:** ₹5,000 (Star), ₹10,000 (Champion), ₹25,000 (Hero)
- **Process:** Submit on HRMS > Recognition module

### 7.2 Annual Awards
- **Employee of the Year:** 1 per department; ₹1,00,000 + trophy + 5 extra EL days
- **Innovator of the Year:** Company-wide; ₹2,00,000 + public recognition
- **Best Team Award:** Top team; team outing budget of ₹50,000

### 7.3 Service Awards
- **5 Years:** ₹10,000 gift voucher + certificate
- **10 Years:** ₹25,000 gift voucher + personalized trophy
- **15 Years:** ₹50,000 gift voucher + 10 days extra EL
- **20+ Years:** ₹1,00,000 + international trip for 2 (5 nights)

---

## 8. 360-DEGREE FEEDBACK

- **Frequency:** Annual (October)
- **Participants:** Self, Manager, 2-3 Peers, 1-2 Direct reports (if applicable), 1 Internal stakeholder
- **Purpose:** Development insights; does NOT directly impact rating but informs it
- **Confidentiality:** Individual responses anonymous; aggregate shared with employee

---

## 9. PROBATION REVIEW

- **Duration:** 6 months from joining
- **Review at 3 months:** Informal check-in by manager
- **Review at 6 months:** Formal probation review by manager + HR
- **Outcomes:** Confirmation / Extension (max 3 months) / Separation
- **Extension notice:** Communicated at least 15 days before probation end date
