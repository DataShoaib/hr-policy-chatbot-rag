# HR Policy Document: Compensation, Payroll & Benefits Policy
## Document ID: HRP-002 | Version: 3.0 | Effective: April 1, 2024

---

## 1. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE

### 1.1 Salary Components (CTC Breakup)

**Fixed Pay Components:**

| Component | Calculation | Taxability |
|-----------|-------------|------------|
| Basic Salary | 40% of CTC | Fully Taxable |
| House Rent Allowance (HRA) | 50% of Basic (Metro) / 40% of Basic (Non-Metro) | Partially Exempt (Sec 10(13A)) |
| Special Allowance | Remaining amount after other components | Fully Taxable |
| Provident Fund (PF) - Employer | 12% of Basic (max ₹1,800/month) | Tax-free |
| Gratuity Provision | 4.81% of Basic | Tax-free at payout |
| Professional Tax | As per state norms | Deductible |

**Variable Pay Components:**
- **Performance Bonus:** 10-25% of Annual CTC based on individual and company performance
- **Joining Bonus:** Offered to select hires; clawback applicable if resignation within 12 months
- **Retention Bonus:** For critical employees; vesting over 2-3 years

### 1.2 Salary Bands (Pay Grades)

| Grade | Level | Annual CTC Range |
|-------|-------|-----------------|
| L1 | Associate | ₹3.5L - ₹6L |
| L2 | Senior Associate | ₹6L - ₹10L |
| L3 | Lead / Specialist | ₹10L - ₹16L |
| L4 | Senior Lead / Manager | ₹16L - ₹25L |
| L5 | Principal / Senior Manager | ₹25L - ₹40L |
| L6 | Director | ₹40L - ₹70L |
| L7 | Senior Director / VP | ₹70L+ |

## 2. PAYROLL PROCESSING

### 2.1 Payroll Calendar
- **Salary Credit Date:** Last working day of each month (or 1st of next month for December)
- **Payslip Generation:** By 25th of each month on HRMS portal
- **Attendance Cutoff:** 20th of each month
- **Attendance for new joiners:** 25th of the joining month

### 2.2 Salary on New Joining
- Salary is paid on pro-rata basis for the number of days worked
- Formula: (Monthly CTC / Number of days in month) × Days worked
- Example: Joining on 15th March = 17 days salary (15th to 31st)

### 2.3 Payroll Deductions

**Statutory Deductions:**
1. **Employee PF:** 12% of Basic Salary (employee contribution)
2. **Professional Tax:** ₹200/month (Maharashtra, Karnataka) / As per state
3. **TDS (Tax Deducted at Source):** Based on tax declaration and Income Tax slabs
4. **ESI (Employees' State Insurance):** 0.75% of Gross Salary (if gross ≤ ₹21,000/month)

**Voluntary Deductions:**
- Voluntary PF contribution (over and above mandatory 12%)
- NPS (National Pension System) - up to 10% of Basic
- Group Health Insurance top-up premium
- Salary advance recovery

---

## 3. PROVIDENT FUND (PF)

### 3.1 PF Contribution
- **Employee Contribution:** 12% of Basic Salary
- **Employer Contribution:** 12% of Basic Salary (split: 8.33% to EPS + 3.67% to EPF)
- **Employer contribution is tax-free up to ₹7,500/month**

### 3.2 PF Withdrawal
- **After 5 years of continuous service:** Full withdrawal with tax exemption
- **Before 5 years:** Taxable as income
- **Partial withdrawal allowed for:** Medical emergency, home purchase, education, marriage

### 3.3 PF Account
- UAN (Universal Account Number) generated at joining
- Employees must link Aadhaar and bank account to UAN
- PF balance check: EPFO portal or UMANG app

---

## 4. GRATUITY

- **Eligibility:** Minimum 5 years of continuous service
- **Formula:** (Last drawn Basic Salary × 15 × Years of Service) / 26
- **Maximum tax-free gratuity:** ₹20 lakhs
- **Payment timeline:** Within 30 days of separation

---

## 5. PERFORMANCE BONUS

### 5.1 Bonus Cycle
- **Annual Performance Bonus:** Paid in April (for previous FY performance)
- **Mid-Year Bonus:** Optional; at company discretion based on business performance

### 5.2 Bonus Calculation

| Performance Rating | Bonus % of Target |
|-------------------|------------------|
| Exceptional (5) | 150% of target |
| Exceeds Expectations (4) | 120% of target |
| Meets Expectations (3) | 100% of target |
| Partially Meets (2) | 60% of target |
| Does Not Meet (1) | 0% |

### 5.3 Bonus Eligibility
- Must be on payroll on bonus payment date
- Employees on PIP are NOT eligible
- Employees serving notice period at time of payment are NOT eligible
- Employees joining after January 1st: Pro-rated bonus based on months worked

## 6. EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

### 6.1 Health Insurance
- **Coverage:** Employee + Spouse + 2 children (up to age 25)
- **Sum Insured:** ₹5 lakhs per family per year (base policy)
- **Top-up available:** Up to ₹20 lakhs (premium shared)
- **Pre-existing conditions:** Covered from Day 1
- **Maternity coverage:** ₹50,000 included in base policy
- **Dental and Vision:** Covered under Wellness Benefit (₹10,000/year)

### 6.2 Term Life Insurance
- **Coverage:** 3x Annual CTC (min ₹50 lakhs)
- **Accidental Death Benefit:** Additional 2x Annual CTC
- **Premium:** Fully paid by company

### 6.3 Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Free counseling sessions (up to 6 per year)
- 24/7 mental health helpline
- Financial planning support

### 6.4 Meal & Travel Allowances
- **Meal Allowance:** ₹2,200/month (tax-exempt under Section 17(2)(viii))
- **Fuel Reimbursement:** ₹15,000/month for L4 and above (with car)
- **Driver Allowance:** ₹9,900/month for L5 and above
- **Phone Reimbursement:** ₹1,000/month (L1-L3), ₹2,000/month (L4+)

### 6.5 Education & Learning Benefits
- **Annual L&D Budget:** ₹30,000/year per employee
- **External Courses:** Reimbursed up to ₹20,000/year with manager approval
- **Professional Certifications:** 100% reimbursed (AWS, Azure, PMP, CFA, etc.)
- **Study Leave:** Up to 5 days per year for exams

### 6.6 Relocation Benefits
- **Domestic Relocation:** Up to 1 month CTC (L1-L3), 2 months CTC (L4+)
- **International Relocation:** As per expat policy (separate document HRP-010)
- **Relocation advance:** Available; recovered in 12 EMIs from salary

## 7. TAX PLANNING & DECLARATIONS

### 7.1 Investment Declaration
- **Submission Deadline:** April 30th of each FY
- **Proof Submission:** January 31st (actual proofs)
- **Common exemptions:** 80C, 80D, HRA, LTA

### 7.2 Form 16
- Issued by **June 15th** each year
- Available on HRMS portal under Tax Documents

### 7.3 Flexi Benefit Plan (FBP)
- Employees can restructure certain components of CTC
- **FBP components:** LTA, Phone, Fuel, Professional Development
- **Declaration deadline:** April 30th

## 8. SALARY REVISION POLICY

- **Annual increment cycle:** April 1st
- **Increment range:** 5-30% based on performance rating and grade
- **Mid-year corrections:** Possible for promotions or market corrections
- **Promotion increments:** Minimum 15% on promotion; may be higher basis band gap
