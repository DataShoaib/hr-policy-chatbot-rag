# HR Policy Document: Recruitment, Hiring & Onboarding Policy
## Document ID: HRP-005 | Version: 2.2 | Effective: January 1, 2024

---

## 1. RECRUITMENT PHILOSOPHY

TechCorp follows a structured, fair, and inclusive hiring process. We hire for both technical competence and cultural alignment. All hiring decisions must be merit-based, bias-free, and comply with equal opportunity guidelines.

---

## 2. OPEN POSITION APPROVAL

### 2.1 Headcount Request Process
1. **Requisition:** Business Manager submits **Manpower Requisition Form (MRF)** on HRMS
2. **Approval Chain:**
   - L1-L3 roles: Department Head → Finance (budget check) → HR
   - L4-L5 roles: Department Head → COO → Finance → HR
   - L6+ roles: CEO → Board (if above budget) → HR
3. **SLA for approval:** 5 business days
4. **Budget verification:** All positions must be in approved annual headcount plan

---

## 3. SOURCING STRATEGY

### 3.1 Internal Job Postings (IJP)
- All positions (L1-L5) must be posted internally for **minimum 7 days** before external search
- **Eligibility for IJP:** Minimum 12 months in current role, no active PIP
- Application via HRMS > Careers > Internal Jobs
- Internal candidates get preference if equally qualified

### 3.2 External Sourcing Channels
1. **Company Career Portal:** careers.techcorp.in (primary channel)
2. **LinkedIn Recruiter:** Used by Talent Acquisition team
3. **Job Boards:** Naukri, Indeed (for L1-L3 roles)
4. **Campus Recruitment:** IITs, NITs, Top MBA colleges (Feb-April)
5. **Employee Referral Program (ERP):** See Section 4
6. **Recruitment Agencies:** Empaneled vendors only; fees negotiated by TA team

### 3.3 Diversity Hiring
- All shortlists for L3+ roles must include minimum 30% women candidates
- Dedicated outreach for SC/ST candidates for government-funded projects
- Diversity hiring report submitted quarterly to Board

## 4. EMPLOYEE REFERRAL PROGRAM (ERP)

### 4.1 Referral Bonus Structure

| Level Hired | Bonus Amount | Payment Timing |
|-------------|-------------|----------------|
| L1-L2 | ₹15,000 | After 3 months of referral joining |
| L3 | ₹25,000 | After 3 months of referral joining |
| L4 | ₹50,000 | After 6 months of referral joining |
| L5+ | ₹1,00,000 | After 6 months of referral joining |

### 4.2 Referral Eligibility Rules
- Referrer must be active employee (not on notice period) at time of bonus payment
- Cannot refer immediate family members (spouse, parents, siblings)
- Cannot refer candidates already in recruitment pipeline
- HR has the right to disqualify a referral if ethical concerns arise

### 4.3 How to Refer
- HRMS > Referral Portal > Submit Candidate Details
- Candidate must mention referrer's name/employee ID in application

## 5. INTERVIEW PROCESS

### 5.1 Standard Interview Rounds

| Level | Rounds |
|-------|--------|
| L1-L2 | 3 rounds (Phone Screen + 2 Technical/HR) |
| L3 | 4 rounds (Screen + 2 Technical + HR + Manager) |
| L4 | 5 rounds (Screen + 3 Technical/Case + HR + Senior Manager) |
| L5+ | 6+ rounds (includes VP/CXO round) |

### 5.2 Interview Guidelines for Interviewers
- **Mandatory training:** All interviewers must complete "Structured Interviewing" course on LMS
- **Structured scoring:** Use STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) framework
- **Feedback submission:** Within 24 hours of interview on HRMS
- **Prohibited questions:** Age, marital status, family planning, religion, caste, disabilities (unless role-relevant accommodation needed)
- **Conflict of interest:** Interviewer must recuse if they know the candidate personally

### 5.3 Interview Expense Reimbursement (for Candidates)
- **Local candidates (same city):** Travel by cab (Ola/Uber) up to ₹500 reimbursed
- **Outstation candidates (L4+):** AC train or economy flight + hotel (1 night max) reimbursed
- Candidate submits receipts to TA team post-interview

## 6. OFFER MANAGEMENT

### 6.1 Offer Approval Authority

| Level | Approval Authority |
|-------|-------------------|
| L1-L3 | HR Manager |
| L4 | HR Director + Department Head |
| L5+ | CHRO + COO |

### 6.2 Offer Letter Contents
- Designation, Grade, CTC (fixed + variable breakup)
- Joining date, location, reporting manager
- Probation period (6 months)
- Notice period terms
- Background verification requirement

### 6.3 Offer Validity
- Offer valid for **7 days** from issue date (L1-L3) / **14 days** (L4+)
- Candidate must digitally sign offer acceptance on HRMS portal

### 6.4 Background Verification (BGV)
- **Mandatory for all hires**
- **BGV Components:** Employment history (last 3 employers), Education (highest degree + last degree), Criminal record check, Address verification
- **BGV Agency:** SecureVerify India (empaneled vendor)
- **Timeline:** 15-20 business days
- **Action on adverse BGV:** Offer revoked or employment terminated (if already joined)

## 7. ONBOARDING PROCESS

### 7.1 Pre-Joining (After Offer Acceptance)
- Welcome email with joining instructions sent within 24 hours
- **Documents required on Day 1:** Offer letter signed, 10th/12th/Graduation certificates, PAN card, Aadhaar card, Bank account details, 2 passport photos, Last 3 months salary slips, Previous employer's relieving letter/experience letter
- Laptop and access setup initiated (IT SLA: 2 business days)

### 7.2 Day 1 Onboarding
**09:00 AM:** Report to HR at Reception
**09:30 AM - 12:30 PM:** Documentation and system access (HR Operations)
**12:30 PM:** Welcome lunch with team
**02:00 PM - 05:00 PM:** Company overview, culture, benefits orientation (HR)

### 7.3 Week 1 Onboarding Schedule
- **Day 2:** IT tools setup, security training (mandatory)
- **Day 3:** Department-specific orientation by manager
- **Day 4:** Meet key stakeholders; buddy assigned
- **Day 5:** First 1:1 with manager; 30-60-90 day plan discussion

### 7.4 30-60-90 Day Plan
- **30 days:** Learn the product, team, tools, and processes
- **60 days:** Start contributing to projects; initial output delivered
- **90 days:** Fully productive; probation check-in with HR

### 7.5 Buddy Program
- Each new joinee gets a "buddy" (peer from same team, not manager)
- Buddy's role: Help with informal questions, cultural navigation, introductions
- Buddy incentive: ₹2,000 gift voucher after 30 days of buddying

### 7.6 Mandatory Trainings (First 30 Days)
1. Information Security Awareness (2 hours, online)
2. POSH Awareness (1 hour, online)
3. Code of Conduct Certification (1 hour, online)
4. Data Privacy & GDPR Basics (1 hour, online)
All trainings available on LMS (lms.techcorp.in)

## 8. REHIRE POLICY

- Employees who left on good terms (rating 3+, no misconduct) eligible for rehire after 6 months gap
- **Previous service credit:** NOT counted for PF, gratuity, or leave purposes
- **Salary:** Treated as fresh hire (no entitlement to previous salary band)
